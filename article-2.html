Consumer To Consumer

Problem 1 : Prone to Scams

Due to the nature of small transactions that are done between individuals, sometimes it is more susceptible to scams. It is so because it is very hard to hold someone’s accountable when you do not have the other party's legal information.
Control Strategy:
Possible causes = Most C2C transaction is done based on trust only
Action to be taken = Performing transaction through a trusted platform

Problem 2 : Problem in claiming warranty

Sometimes, a private seller does not know the items that he sells in and out. Thus resulting in some problems when the unfortunate happens which causes the buyer to raise up a dispute and wants to claim warranty.
Control Strategy:
Possible causes = Sometimes, private seller is not knowledgeable on things they sell
Action to be taken = Seller should know the bare minimum information regarding their items and provide a clear warranty policy to their buyer

Consumer To Business

Problem 1 : Might Be Unpredictable

C2B model could be a risk in the long term because as a business owner, you can not control if you’re betting on a customer’s marketing efficacy.
Control Strategy:
Possible causes = Unpredictable outcome for long term.
Action to be taken = Control and report any C2B model.

Problem 2 : Negative Consumer Responses

Business owner must be prepared to mitigate any criticism without shutting down the channels of connection you’ve established with your consumers who provide your company value.
Control Strategy:
Possible causes = Unprofessional consumers on things they sell.
Action to be taken = Evaluating the procedure for recruiting more professional consumers.

Business To Consumer

Problem 1 : Depending on Consumer

Having customers has a big influence on sales and also provides everything for the company and maintains the company's survival.
Control Strategy:
Possible causes = Unable to stabilize sales
Action to be taken = Offers attractive offers, for example buy 2 get 1 free

Problem 2 : Customers will not visit the platform to shop

Control Strategy:
Possible causes = Not attractive and eye catching.
Action to be taken = Must optimize their platform so that customers visit the seller's site

Business To Business

Problem 1 : Maintaining Customer Relationships With B2B E-Commerce

Control Strategy:
Possible causes = B2B buyers would choose to buy from a supplier that they had a great relationship with even if the competition’s business terms were more favorable.
Action to be taken = Educating your sales teams

Problem 2 : Finding The Right Market And Competitor Analysis

Control Strategy:
Possible causes = Not understanding the right market and competitors in the market
Action to be taken = Conduct a strong competitor analysis

Planning Control Approach

We choose Strategic Aspect Approach, becuase it mainly deals with policies. When a business have a good policy and implement it throughout their practice, it will reduce the likelihood of risk happening improve the business performance.
