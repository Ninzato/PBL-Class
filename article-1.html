PBL 1: Risk Inventory Preparation for PBL Group's Daycare

PBL Daycare implements all six IT compontents into its business to keep up with the ever changing world. It is proven to be a useful approach as it helps the business tremendously and increase the revenue by approximately 200% from the previous year. Unfortunately, the change does not only bring in positive impacts to the company. Along with the benefits that come, various risks also started to put their eyes on the business and threaten to damage it. In this ocassion, our trusted Audit Team will share with you guys some of the risk that our business encountered and what risk control that we implement to keep the risks in check and keep the company running well.
